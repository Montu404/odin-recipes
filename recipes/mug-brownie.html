2 minute brownie in a mug

One mug, quick bake mug brownie

I've found this mug cake while in a pretty bad mental spot and ever since it's been my go to quick treat.
It's also made with stuff you probably already have in your pantry, so it's really ideal in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp milk

Steps

  1. Add the egg and the rest of the ingredients, mixing after every item.
  2. Mix exerything thoroughly.
  3. Put it in the microwave for ~3 minutes, or until not wet on top.
  4. Enjoy!
